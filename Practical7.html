Ratan Tata

Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist and former chairman of Tata group. He is known for his leadership and contribution to Indian industry.Ratan Naval Tata is one of India’s most respected business leaders and philanthropists. He served as the Chairman of the Tata Group from 1991 to 2012 and briefly again in 2016–2017. Under his leadership, the group expanded globally and became one of the most powerful business groups in the world.
